Support

Free Support

If you have a problem, start by reviewing the Quick-Start Guide and the FAQ. If you're getting an unexpected exception, read the text of the exception carefully: we often use exceptions to explain improper use of NUnitAsp.

If the NUnitAsp documentation doesn't help you, look through the archives of the SourceForge trackers and mailing list.

Finally, if you don't find an answer in any of these places, post your question to a tracker or the nunitasp-users mailing list. Please do not email the programmers directly. Posting your question archives the discussion so others can refer to it later.
