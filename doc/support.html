Support

SourceForge Trackers

SourceForge has bug, feature, and support request tools. If our documentation doesn't meet your needs, post an issue to one of the SourceForge trackers.

Mailing List

You can subscribe to the NUnitAsp mailing list at SourceForge. On the list we talk about problems with NUnitAsp and plan new developments. There's also a message archive on the web site. You're welcome to post your issue here instead of using the trackers. Please do not email the programmers directly. Posting to the list archives the discussion so others can refer to it later.

Service-Level Agreements

We may provide service-level agreements if there is enough demand. If you are interested in purchasing an SLA, contact Jim Little.

On-Site Training and Consulting

Jim Little, the project coordinator for NUnitAsp, provides on-site training and consulting. He's responsible for the majority of the NUnitAsp codebase and knows it inside and out. If your organization needs a little extra help with NUnitAsp, check out our on-site training and consulting offerings.
