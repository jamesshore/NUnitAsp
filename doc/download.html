Download

We have versions of NUnitAsp for NUnit 1.x and NUnit 2.0.

  • For NUnit 2.0, our current release is Version 1.2.1. Download it here.

  • For NUnit 1.x, our current release is Version 1.2. Download it here. Other than the version of NUnit and a few minor documentation changes, there's no difference between NUnitAsp v1.2 and v1.2.1. NUnitAsp v1.2 will be the last version of NUnitAsp to support NUnit 1.x.

After downloading, be sure to follow the instructions in the Installation Guide. Before creating your first test, skim the checklist in the Quick Start Guide.

To be notified of new releases, subscribe to our announcements mailing list. This is a very low volume list.
